This document provides guidelines for achieving the best fine-tuned model for Classification by focusing on refining data quality, including text cleaning, number of examples, balance of examples per class, length of texts, deduplication, and creating a high-quality test set. It also offers a troubleshooting guide for fine-tuned models.
