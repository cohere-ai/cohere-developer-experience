Today, we're pleased to announce that we fine-tuning is now available for Command R 08-2024!

We're also introducing other fine-tuning features:
- Support for longer context lengths for fine-tuning training and MultiLoRA.
- Integration with Weights & Biases for tracking fine-tuning experiments in real time.

You can find more information in this [blog post](https://cohere.com/blog/fine-tuning-command0824).
